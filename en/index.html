Dropp Pro · Double Tap

One gesture, without touching the screen.

Pinch your thumb and index finger twice: depending on the screen, DROPP starts the infusion, pauses it, resumes it, or acknowledges an alert. Hands full, hands gloved — the Apple Watch's native gesture answers at the wrist, no contact needed.

Straight answers

What stays free?

Unlimited calculations, the visual tempo, tracking for one infusion, and the end alarm — no ads, no account, no time limit.

Does DROPP work offline?

Yes, entirely. Everything is computed and stored on the device; no connection is ever needed.

Is the iPhone required with the Apple Watch?

Yes — the Watch app comes with the iPhone app and installs through it. After that, wrist alarms ring even if the iPhone is off or out of range.

Which drop factors are available?

10, 15, 20 and 60 drops per milliliter — the factor can be changed at any time in Settings.

What is Tap Mode for?

Reproducing an observed rhythm by tapping the screen to read its frequency per minute — and, for a drip rate, its mL/h. No stopwatch, no mental math.

Does DROPP replace a pump or a control device?

No. DROPP is an aid for calculation, rhythm and tracking. It measures nothing, decides nothing, and substitutes for no device or protocol.

Which versions are compatible?

iOS 17 or later, watchOS 10 or later.
